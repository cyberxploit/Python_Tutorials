				
***INTRODUCTION TO DATABASE***


CHARACTERS: This are the combinations of alphabets, numbers and symbols

DATA: A data is a collection of raw facts(information that is not processed that is in form characters).

RECORDS: This are the collections or combinations of data(row in a database)

FILES: It is the coming together of two or more records

DATABASE: A database is the collections of files and related records

DATABASE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM: DBMS is a software package designed to define, manipulate, retrieve and manage data in database.
